Field Notes - Borneo Rainforest Expedition 2024
Date: March 12-28, 2024
Observer: Dr. S. Patel, Wildlife Conservation Society

Day 1: Proboscis monkey troop (11 individuals) observed at riverside foraging zone.
Day 3: Pygmy elephant tracks documented near natural salt lick at GPS 5.2103N, 117.4432E.
Day 7: Orangutan nest count: 34 active nests recorded in 2 km transect.
Day 12: Sun bear claw marks identified on Dipterocarp tree at elevation 340 m.
Day 14: Collection of faecal samples from 8 individual sambar deer for dietary analysis.
